Clock-In:

  1. Open POS.
  2. Clock In.

Policies:

  1. Label any customers sensitive parts with Work Order # for example: Hard Drive, SSD, or any part customer noted they may want back.
  2. SSDs & Hard Drives are to be placed in Static Bags for customers return.
  3. If device is not completed and you won't be here next day, Use the back of "QC Sheet" and jot down notes to have updated.

Assessment:

  1. Diagnose Computer to the Best of your ability & update Parts Need Replace.
  2. Verify stock (if low or out of stock, check box next to item needing to be replaced).

Approval Process:

  1. Compare Approvals to your diagnostics.
  2. If additional parts need to be replaced put computer back together and send to Sales Bar for customer approval or if Approvals match continue repair.

Final Test:

  1. Complete Final Tests.
  2. If additional parts are needed, repeat Approval process.

Clean:

  1. Clean computer to the best of your ability and return to Sales Bar.

Charge Computer & Stress Test:

  1. Charge computer to at least 50%.
  2. During Youtube 4k playback, run All Tests on Cinebench.
  3. Restart computer and PRAM zap (resets startup disk to internal default).
