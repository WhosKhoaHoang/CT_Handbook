Low Inventory Notice:

  1. Physically verify low or no inventory.
  2. Click on "Inventory" tab.
  3. Click on "Item List".
  4. Enter "Item #" or "Item Name" within Search Bar.
  5. Within "Item Detail" Click on "Orders" verify no "Purchase Orders" exist.
  6. Within "Item Detail" go to "History Tab".
  7. Double Click on most recent "Receive Item".
  8. Now you will have "Vendor Name" & "Yellow tab #" near bottom of page.
  9. If this item cannot be reordered from same vendor, contact "Minh La" for assistance.

Placing Order With Vendor:

  1. Go to Vendors website.
  2. Use "Yellow tab #" in Vendors website to reference previous ordered item.
  3. If this item cannot be reordered from same vendor, contact "Minh La" for assistance.

Create New Purchase Order:

  1. Click on "Purchase" tab for drop down menu.
  2. Click on "New Purchase Order".
  3. Input "Vendor Name" on the top left.
  4. Input "Item #" or "Item Name" in "Enter Item(s)" bar.
  5. If there is a shipping charge add it to the total item cost.
  6. Include "Yellow tab #" within Instructions Section on the bottom left of PO.
  7. When completed Click on "Save Only"
  8. All done.
