Prep Drop-off Bag:

  1. Reference "Smart Pick-up & Drop-off Sheet" with what's in the bag.
  2. Take pick up sheets & drop off sheets.
  3. Take parts needed for Onsite Repairs.
  4. Map out your drive and update customers with ETAs.

Drop-off Procedures:

  1. Drop off items and read description repairs and total paid.
  2. Receive drop-off signatures.

Pick-up Procedures:

  1. Match item with Serial # provided on "Pick Up Worksheet"
  2. Receive pick-up signatures.
  3. If serial # does not match use "NR Pick Up Worksheet".
  4. Receive pick-up signatures.
