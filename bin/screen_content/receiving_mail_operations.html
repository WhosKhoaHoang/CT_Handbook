Incoming Parts

  1. Click on "Purchasing".
  2. Click on "Purchase Order List".
  3. Match Incoming Parts within "Waiting on Parts" Computers.
  4. If not waiting on computer, reference the Qty of the parts to Open Purchases to help narrow down the PO. Computers.
  5. If part was ordered for a Work Order, place part within "Waiting on Parts" Computer Order and follow Update Work Order Procedures.
  6. When PO is found, Note down "Bin#" (you'll need it for putting parts away).
  7. Click on "I want to" drop down & "Receive Items".
  8. Click "Select All" , Check "Include Instructions", & click "Continue".
  9. Click "Save Only"
  10. Place parts into correct Bin# in "Warehouse".

Incoming Computer into POS

  1. Unbox items and reference Company or Customer Name.
  2. Bring in Computer by reference Customer Name or Company Name.

Bring in Parts

  1. Match Incoming Parts within PO List
  2. Match Qty w/ Purchase Orders
  3. Receive Item within Purchase Orders
  4. Place parts into Warehouse Inventory
