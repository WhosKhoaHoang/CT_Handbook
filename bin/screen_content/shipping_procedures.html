Apply Shipping to 1 Work Order

  1. If multiple devices need to be shipped for 1 company, remove shipping charges from all work order other than 1.

Shipping Label:

  1. Open "Endicia Application"
  2. Reference Company address within "Mailing Log".
  3. You can search "Company Name" in search.
  4. Reference the last shipment. "Right Click" & "New shipment from selection".
  5. Each device should have an Estimate weight of 7lbs. If 2 devices total: 14lbs.
  6. Change Mailpiece to "Large Flat Rate Box".
  7. Make sure "Email" address is associated to company shipment.
  8. Print Shipping Label by clicking "Prepare Label for Printing".
  9. Open "Mailing Log" & email customer's last shipment information.
  10. Change Subject text to "Package Shipped To You from CleverTech!" & "Send Mail".

Group Multiple Devices & Packaging:

  1. If a company has multiple devices that need to be shipped group them in batches of two. Two devices should be able to fit in "Flat Rate Large Box" safely.
  2. Place up to 2 devices in "Yellow Box". Make sure laptop screen faces inwards.
  3. Prep "Flat Rate Large Box" by "Sealing" & adding padding to 1 end.
  4. Place "Yellow Box" (with up to 2 devices) inside "Flat Rate Large Box".
  5. Add padding to the end of box and seal box.

Apply Shipping Label:

  1. Apply printed Shipping Label.
  2. Place item in "TO GO Mail Bin"
